Financial Market Visualizations

This page displays interactive visualizations of currency exchange rates against the Malaysian Ringgit (MYR) and market comparisons of companies in Malaysia.

Currency Exchange Rates against MYR

This visualization displays a world map where each country is colored according to its currency exchange rate against the MYR over time.

Company Sales, Profit & Market Value

This scatter plot compares the sales, profit, and market value of Malaysian companies, with interactive features such as sliders for filtering data based on various metrics.

This visualisation is created by Chung Min Yi. The data sources are from: Malaysia's Largest Companies Dataset and Currency Exchange Rates from data.gov.my.
